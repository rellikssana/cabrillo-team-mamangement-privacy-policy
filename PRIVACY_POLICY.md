## Introduction

Cabrillo Coastal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Cabrillo Coastal Team Management Chrome browser extension ("Extension" or "Service").

**IMPORTANT: This Extension is for internal company use only and is intended exclusively for Cabrillo Coastal employees and authorized team members. This is not a public extension and access is restricted to authorized personnel.**

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data as an employee or authorized team member.

By using the Extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and in compliance with Cabrillo Coastal's internal policies and applicable employment agreements.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information when you use the Extension:

1. **Account Information**
   - Email address
   - Password (encrypted and stored securely)
   - Full name
   - User role (User or Admin)

2. **Schedule and Activity Data**
   - Assigned schedules
   - Shift times and dates
   - Activity assignments
   - Status updates
   - Task confirmations

3. **Usage Data**
   - Extension usage patterns
   - Login timestamps
   - Session information
   - Notification interactions

4. **Team Communication Data**
   - Team updates and announcements
   - Status change history
   - Schedule modification records

### Automatically Collected Information

The Extension may automatically collect certain information, including:
- Browser type and version
- Extension version
- Device information
- IP address (for security and authentication purposes)
- Timestamps of activities

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Primary Uses

1. **Service Provision**
   - To provide and maintain the Extension's functionality

   - To display your schedules and team information
   - To enable team communication and updates

2. **Schedule Management**
   - To assign and manage team schedules
   - To track status updates and activity changes
   - To send notifications about upcoming tasks
   - To coordinate team activities

3. **Team Coordination**
   - To facilitate team communication
   - To share team updates and announcements
   - To enable administrators to manage team members
   - To provide team overview and status monitoring

4. **System Functionality**
   - To maintain and improve the Extension
   - To ensure security and prevent fraud
   - To troubleshoot technical issues
   - To analyze usage patterns for service improvement

### Administrative Uses

1. **User Management**
   - To manage user accounts and permissions
   - To assign roles (User or Admin)
   - To maintain team member lists

2. **Data Analysis**
   - To generate reports and analytics
   - To understand usage patterns
   - To improve service functionality

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party cloud database service. Data is stored in secure servers with appropriate security measures in place.

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

- **Encryption**: Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods
- **Secure Connections**: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access control ensures only authorized users can access data
- **Authentication**: Secure authentication mechanisms protect user accounts
- **Regular Updates**: Security updates are applied regularly to maintain protection

### Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:
- Provide the Extension's services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

When you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal information in accordance with applicable laws, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

## Browser Permissions

The Extension requires the following browser permissions:

### Required Permissions

1. **Storage**: To store session information and user preferences locally
2. **Notifications**: To send browser notifications about schedules and updates
3. **Alarms**: To schedule and manage notification timers
4. **ActiveTab**: To interact with the current browser tab when needed
5. **Scripting**: To provide dynamic functionality
6. **Tabs**: To manage browser tabs for dashboard access
7. **Windows**: To manage browser windows
8. **Offscreen**: For background operations

### Host Permissions

- **Supabase Domain**: To communicate with Supabase servers for authentication and data storage

These permissions are necessary for the Extension to function properly. We do not use these permissions for any purpose other than providing the Extension's services.
